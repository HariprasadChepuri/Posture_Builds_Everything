Bonus Health Tips For Everyone
  • Drink more water in morning,less at night.
  • Best sleeping time is 10pm to 4am.
  • Limit TV, computer,etc time to less than 2 hours per day.
  • Let kids help plan and prepare 1 meal each week.
  • Try to get between 30 and 60 minutes of physical activity each day.
  • Try not to overeat.
  • Reward children with praise, not food.
  • Put workouts on your calendar so you keep them.
  • Answer phone calls from left ear.
  • Dont eat heavy meals after 5pm.
  • Take the stairs instead of the elevator.
  • Exercise while watching television at home. Use a machine, lift weights, and stretch.
  • Eat more whole grains
