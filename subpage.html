Sitting
Poor posture can lead to
  1. Spine Curvature
  2. Back Pain, neck pain and headache
  3. Can lead to digestive issues
To maintain correct posture
  1. Sit up with your back straight
  2. Your shoulders back
  3. Your buttocks should touch the back of your chair


On bed
Poor posture can lead to
  1. Aggravate neck or back pain
  2. Obstructive sleep apnea
  3. Alzheimer's or Parkinson's
To maintain correct posture
  1. Try to keep your position as flat as possible when sleeping on your back


Cleaning
Poor posture can lead to
  1. Neck or Back pain
  2. Knee pain
  3. Unwanted strain on your upper and lower back
To maintain correct posture
  1. Stand as straight as possible
  2. Avoid being in the same position for a long time


Nutrition
Poor posture can lead to
  1. Slouched posture after or while a meal can trigger heartburn caused by acid reflux
  2. Lack of interest in taking nutrition
To maintain correct posture
  1. The goal is a neutral, upright spine position - not flexed too far forward or backward
